Lasagna Special

Lasagna is a wide, flat pasta shape and possibly one of the oldest types of pasta. Our classic recipe features a rich, slow-cooked Bolognese sauce, creamy Bechamel, and a generous blend of Parmesan and Mozzarella cheese.

The history of lasagna traces back to ancient Naples. Building a perfect lasagna is like constructing a masterpiece—it’s all about the balance of layers. We use handmade egg pasta sheets, rolled so thin they are almost translucent.

Our secret is the five-hour simmering process of the meat sauce. We use a blend of high-quality beef and pork, slowly reduced with red wine and aromatic herbs. This creates a deep, savory flavor that is truly unforgettable.

To finish, we bake it until the top layer forms a golden-brown, crispy crust. This "crosta" protects the delicate layers underneath while providing a satisfying crunch. We hope this dish brings a piece of Italian sunshine to your table. Enjoy your meal!
